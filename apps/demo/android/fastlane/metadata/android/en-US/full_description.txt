Web of Trust lets you build a decentralized digital identity — verified through real, face-to-face encounters.

No central authority. No corporate accounts. Just people verifying each other.

Features:

* Create your self-sovereign cryptographic identity (BIP39 seed, Ed25519 keys)
* Verify contacts in person via QR code scanning
* Issue and receive attestations ("I know this person")
* Share data in encrypted group spaces with real-time sync
* Works offline — sync when you reconnect
* Multi-device support — same identity across all your devices

Your keys never leave your device. All data that leaves your device is encrypted and signed. Built on open standards (did:key, HKDF, Yjs CRDTs).

Web of Trust is free and open-source software (MIT license).
